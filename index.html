ii. How I work
one
Understand

Stakeholder interviews, desk research, and user observation to define the real problem — not the assumed one.

two
Structure

User flows, site maps, and low-fidelity wireframes to establish architecture before reaching for colour or polish.

three
Prototype

Interactive Figma or ProtoPie prototypes with realistic transitions, tested with real users in moderated sessions.

four
Iterate

Synthesis of findings into actionable changes. I document decisions so teams can understand the reasoning, not just the outcome.
